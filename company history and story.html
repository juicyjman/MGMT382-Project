Company Story and History

Origin of Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven

The story of the Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven Co. can be traced back to 2003, when two hang-gliding passionates met on a hang gliding ride at the North Carolina State. Both Harry and Harriet enjoy flying in the sky and want to provide people with the opportunities to view the world from the point of view of an free-flying eagle. After years of experiences of hang gliding, Harry and Harriet started developing business model and made a huge success with their special operation method. Currently, we have expanded our landscape in three different states and we are expecting to have more!
